Our Story

Har Rasoi ki Pehli Pasand

JNA Groups was built on one simple belief — good food starts with good ingredients, and good garlic shouldn't cost you time or tears.

Why We Exist

Peeling garlic is one of the most repetitive, time-consuming jobs in any kitchen — whether it's a home cook prepping dinner or a restaurant kitchen racing through a lunch rush. JNA Groups exists to remove that one step, without compromising on freshness or purity.

Based in Varanasi, we supply 100% pure, hygienically peeled garlic — no preservatives, no chemical whitening, no shortcuts. Just garlic, peeled and packed the way it should be.

What started as a way to serve local households has grown into a supply chain that now serves street vendors, restaurants, and premium hotel kitchens across the region — each with sourcing consistency and reliable delivery.

Sourcing

Where Our Garlic Comes From

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Our home base and primary local sourcing & processing hub — where garlic is peeled, sorted, and packed for regional supply.

Mandsaur–Neemuch Belt, Madhya Pradesh

India's leading garlic-growing region, supplying the bulk of raw garlic that feeds our processing pipeline for larger orders.
